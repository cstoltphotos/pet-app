About Me

- Carter Stolt goes to the University of Maine and is majoring in Computer Science (he coded this website) and Business Administration. He picked up photography after he bought a camera for his high school film club, which he was president of for two years. Film had always interested Carter and his experience in that made the transition to photography easier than other beginners may have found it.
- Carter now travels with his camera all over New England, especially on hikes, and has started his own photography business, hoping to save towards his long-term dream of opening a Cat Cafe!
- Carter loves working with people, pets, and the environment to perfect photos and build community. Apart from photography, Carter tutors and tour-guides at UMaine, is vice-president of the UMaine Volleyball Club, and attends meetings of the Microbiology Society!
